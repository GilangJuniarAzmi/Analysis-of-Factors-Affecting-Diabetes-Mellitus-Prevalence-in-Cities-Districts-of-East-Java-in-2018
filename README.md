# Analysis-of-Factors-Affecting-Diabetes-Mellitus-Prevalence-in-Cities-Districts-of-East-Java-in-2018
This study aims to raise awareness among the government and public opinion about preventing and controlling diabetes mellitus. Data used in this study obtained from the East Java Basic Health (Riskesdas) Research in 2018. Based on the results of the study found that the consumption of fatty foods, the population that has never checked blood sugar levels, the nutritional status based on the body mass index (BMI) category with the characteristics of obesity, as well as the population who has never consumed fruit and vegetables were the most influential factors in the increase in the prevalence of diabetes mellitus in East Java in 2018 with an adjusted R-squared value of 79%.
